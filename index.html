About

Emergencysos was inspired by the need for a centralized platform that provides emergency contacts for people in East Africa. We believe that everyone, regardless of their nationality or location, deserves access to essential services during emergencies.

This project was developed as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, a coding bootcamp that focuses on software engineering and technology. The timeline for this project was approximately 2 months, from planning and design to implementation and testing.

We are a team of dedicated developers who are passionate about making a positive impact on the lives of people in need. Each member of the team has a unique background and skill set, which has contributed to the success of this project.
